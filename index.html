The four pillars

Everything a blader needs, in one app.

Live battle map

Open the app and see who's hosting a battle near you right now. Pins show the format, skill level, open spots, and host profile — tap to request a spot.

  • Real-time session pins across HK and TW
  • Filter by format: Burst, X, Metal, Plastic Gen, HMS
  • See host rating and battle history before you join

Host a session anywhere

Battling at a park, a shop, or a friend's place? Drop a pin, set the format and spot count, and wait for nearby bladers to request to join. You approve the roster, lock it, and start.

  • Choose location, format, and max players
  • Approve or decline join requests
  • In-session chat opens automatically when the roster locks

Real-time battle chat

Every session gets its own private chat. Coordinate arrival, share launch videos, and talk shop before, during, and after the battle. Photos supported.

  • Private per-session chat room
  • Photos and reactions supported
  • Notifications when the host starts the battle

Collection & reputation

Track every Beyblade you own — Plastic Gen, HMS, Metal, Burst, X — and show off rare parts on your profile. After each battle, rate your opponents on sportsmanship to build community reputation.

  • Organize your collection by generation
  • Photo-first collection cards
  • Post-battle rating with sportsmanship score

Frequently asked

Questions bladers ask.

Is 321GoShoot free?

Yes. The app is free to download and use. There are no subscriptions and no ads. We may add optional features later but the core — map, sessions, chat, AI identifier, collection, ratings — stays free.

Which devices are supported?

iPhone running iOS 16 and later. iPad works but the app is optimized for iPhone. Android is not supported yet.

Do I need to be in Hong Kong or Taiwan to use it?

You can download it anywhere, but the active community is currently in HK and TW. Sessions outside those regions may be sparse at launch. The app auto-switches your map to your user region, and you can toggle between HK and TW in Settings.

How does the AI identifier work?

You tap "Identify" and the photo is sent over HTTPS to our Cloud Function, which forwards it to Gemini 2.5 Flash. The response (product name + short reasoning) comes back in your chosen language. Photos aren't stored on our side unless you add the Beyblade to your collection.

Can I delete my account?

Yes. Profile → Settings (gear icon) → Delete Account. This removes your profile, sessions, photos, and collection from our servers permanently.

How do I report a user or content?

Every profile, session, image, and chat message has a three-dot menu with Report and Block actions. Reports are reviewed within 24 hours. Blocks are symmetric — neither side sees the other's content.
